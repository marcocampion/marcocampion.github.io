Research Interests

My area of research focuses on formal methods. In particular, my main area of expertise is Abstract Interpretation, a mathematical theory to design program semantics, program properties and program analysis. I am also interested in foundational aspects of computation, with particular attention to the relation between intensional and extensional properties of code.

Research topics:

  • Abstract Interpretation
  • Program Analysis
  • Program Verification
  • Computability and Formal Languages
